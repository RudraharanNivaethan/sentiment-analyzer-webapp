Welcome to Sentiment Analysis App

This web application helps you analyze the sentiment behind any text you provide. It uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to measure:

  • Compound Score: An overall sentiment score ranging from -1 (most negative) to +1 (most positive).
  • Overall Sentiment: Categorized as Positive , Negative , or Neutral based on the compound score.
  • Subjectivity: A value from 0 (very objective) to 1 (very subjective), showing how much the text expresses opinion vs fact.
  • Sentiment Breakdown: Percentage of positive, neutral, and negative words in the text.
